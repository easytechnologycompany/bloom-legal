You can request deletion of your Bloom account and its associated data at any time. This page explains how, and what data is removed or kept.

How to request account deletion

  1. From the email address associated with your Bloom account, send an email to easytechcopmany@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my Bloom account".
  3. Include the phone number registered to your account so we can verify it.

We verify the request comes from the account owner and complete the deletion within 30 days. We'll email you to confirm once it's done.

What data is deleted

  • Your account and profile (name, email, phone number)
  • Saved delivery addresses
  • Favorites, wishlist, and recently viewed items
  • Your order history and any personal notes attached to orders

What may be kept, and for how long

  • Transaction and order records required for legal, tax, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes may be retained for up to 7 years, as required by applicable law. These are retained in a limited form and are not used to identify you for any other purpose.
  • Aggregated or anonymized data that can no longer be linked to you may be retained.

If you have any questions about deleting your account or data, contact us at easytechcopmany@gmail.com.
